Why you should report

Phishing

(simulations)

Reporting phishing emails, whether they’re simulated or not, can have a direct impact on your company’s cybersecurity.

Here’s why.

Spreading awareness

The sooner your IT administrators are aware of a targeted phishing campaign, the sooner they can take preventive measures. Many attacks can be personalised or specifically aimed at your organisation, which makes them very convincing. Reporting these gives your colleagues a head start.

Training your critical eye

When you look at possible phishing messages with the intent to report suspicious activity, you’ll automatically be more critical of potentially fishy elements, such as hyperlinks or senders’ email addresses.

You’re on the hook: always report phishing attempts!
